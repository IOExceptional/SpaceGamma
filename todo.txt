THEME: Entire game on one screen

Game Type:

Space sim



Potential ideas:

Objectives for the entire game drawn on the screen




Objectives:

Get cargo from the space station to the outpost




Gameplay:

You undock from a space station with a special load in the cargo bay.

It's important that this cargo makes it to the outpost on the other side of the planet

Space pirates have been alerted to this cargo movement.

You have been armed with a laser gun, defend yourself and make it to the outpost alive!!
